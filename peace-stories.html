PEACEKEEPING STORIES


Peacekeeping is not a task but our solemn duty, which we are bound to fulfil. We must pledge to maintain peace in every small way which may help our community. This responsibility has been bestowed to us by the Divine and one must obey it, regardless of social or cultural barriers.

Some notable people have long strived to maintain peace in society. Here are a couple of them:

NELSON MANDELA

Nelson Mandela suffered imprisonment for 27 years yet his resolve was unshaken. He united South Africa and dismantled apartheid to its roots.

MARTIN LUTHER KING JR

Martin Luther King Jr's iconic speech 'I Have A Dream...' is a symbol of his integrity and dedication to foster peace and abolish racism.
